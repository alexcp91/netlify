Why Netlify?

I came across Netlify in the same manner, I imagine, as many of your customers: by growing frustrated with the complexity of hosting a website and searching for a new way. Until I learned about the JAMstack, I did not appreciate there was an alternative to Ruby on Rails, Django or the MERN stack. It took some time to understand how and why Netlify was different. But, it wasn't long until my "ah-ha" moment.

Netlify has built a fundamentally different way to deliver the modern web. In hindsight, the traditional web architecture, where pages are built for each request, is clearly overly complex, insecure and inefficient. I'm a 100% self-taught coder, so I appreciate abstraction in favor of ease-of-use, efficiency and security. With each passing year, the complexity to build a website has only increased. In 2018, I shouldn't have to understand Docker and Kubernetes to launch a fast and secure web application. This is the complexity Netlify elegantly addresses, and that is what excites me about the company. It's is natural evolution to Wordpress. Netlify has the potential to power the world's websites and it's well on its way.

That's an adventure I'd be excited to join.
